## 📌 Problem Statement

Crypto traders on Hyperliquid face significant losses during
high-volatility sentiment periods. Without data-driven risk
analysis, traders continue using high leverage during
Extreme Fear market conditions — leading to maximum losses.

---

## 💡 Solution

A sentiment-driven trade intelligence dashboard that analyzes
35,000+ real crypto trades, identifies loss patterns across
market sentiment zones, and delivers actionable risk reduction
strategies for traders and platforms like Primetrade.ai.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 📉 **Sentiment-Loss Correlation** — identifies highest loss periods
- ⚠️ **Risk Zone Dashboard** — visual risk monitoring by sentiment
- 📊 **Leverage Analysis** — data-driven leverage recommendations
- 🔍 **Pattern Detection** — trade behavior across Fear/Greed zones
- 💡 **Strategy Recommendations** — 40% leverage reduction insight

---

## 📊 Key Findings

- Highest losses consistently occur during **Extreme Fear** periods
- Traders using high leverage during fear zones lose significantly more
- Recommended **40% leverage reduction** during Extreme Fear periods
- Built risk-zone dashboard for real-time trade monitoring
